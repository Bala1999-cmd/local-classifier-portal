Pollachi Shanmugasundaram

Shanmugasundaram, 44, of Samarayapatti in Madathukulam in Tirupur, has filed a total of four nomination papers and if one were to be rejected for some reason, election officials could consider the remaining. His wife K Vanitha, 44, a homemaker, has filed nomination as the party’s substitute candidate for the constituency.

In his affidavit, the candidate said his family has movable assets worth Rs 3.04 crore. It said he has Rs 5.21 lakh cash in hand, 50% shares in Anusam Knitters Pvt Ltd, 50% shares in Southern Star Coir Products Pvt Ltd and 332g of gold ornaments. As per the affidavit, his wife has Rs 4.05 lakh cash in hand, 1kg and 120g of gold ornaments and 7,250g of silver articles. Shanmugasundaram told the media that he would take steps to implement the long-pending Anaimalai-Nallaru project and to provide train facilities to various parts of the country from Pollachi. “More trains will be operated between Palani and Coimbatore. I will also take steps to increase tea estate workers’ wages, besides pitching for a special court to conduct trail on the sexual harassment case.” Shanmugasundaram had in 2009 contested from Pollachi but lost to the AIADMK candidate K Sugumar. He was not given the party ticket in the 2014 general elections when the party fielded former minister Pongalur N Palanisamy, who lost to the AIADMK’s C Mahendran.
